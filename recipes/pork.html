Fried pork

Description

Recipe for fried pork Serves 6. Great weekend meal!1.

List of Ingredients needed

  • 4kg of pork rib
  • 8 Tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • Stoke cube
  • 5 chillies
  • 2 Teaspoon of salt
  • Palm sized chunck of root ginger
  • Soy sauce

    • For the sides

  • 1 Green carbage
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 4 medium ppotatoes
  • 4 matooke fingers

Cooking Method

  1. Firstly, make sure the pork ribs are cut into bite-size and skewerable-size pieces. Ask the butcher to do this.

  2. Place a large saucepan over high heat and add a little oil. Once sizzling add the pork and fry for a couple of minutes. Add roughly chopped tomatoes, onions, stock cube and salt and fry together for a further 10 minutes. Use a spatula to encourage the tomatoes to break up and create a paste in the pan.
