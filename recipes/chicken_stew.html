Chicken Stew

Description

A stew like no other. It's easy, aromatic and flavorful with a thick rich roasted based chicked flavour sauce. Great weekend meal!1.

List of Ingredients needed

  • 1 Fryer cut up chicken
  • 4 Tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 1 Large sliced Onion
  • 4 Cloves of garlic
  • 3 Tomatoes. peeled and cut
  • 2 Irish potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 Teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of Royco
  • 1 Teaspoon of Paprika
  • 2 teaspoons of chicken masala
  • 1/2 Teaspoon pepper (optional)

Cooking Method

  1. You can either use pre-roasted enkoko enkalirire or not pre-roasted.

  2. Heat oil, add garlic when it develops a good flavor then add the onions until soft and golden brown.

  3. Add tomatoes and salt, stir well and cook until soft.

  4. Add all the spices, stir well.

  5. Add the chicken pieces, stir well and mix with the content in the sauce pan and cover for some time.

  6. Stir occassionally to prevent sticking

  7. Cook until it develops a good flavor,then add stock or water just a little above the chicken, or enough to stir thoroughly well.

  8. Cover and let to boil on medium heat

  9. When it boils reduce temperature or reduce to simmering temperatures (slow boiling from rapid boiling).

  10. Cook until cooked/ready and tender.

  11. Serve Hot
